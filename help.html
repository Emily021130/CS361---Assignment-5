Help & Documentation

Welcome to the Hotel Booking System. Here is a guide to using our platform:

1. Registering an Account

To book a room, you must first register an account.

2. Logging In

If you already have an account, log in to access your cart and bookings.

3. Viewing Available Rooms

On the home page, you can browse the available rooms and their prices.

4. Adding Rooms to Your Cart

Click the "Add to Cart" button to temporarily store rooms before booking.

5. Viewing Your Cart

Check your selected rooms in the Cart
